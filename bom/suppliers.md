Chair-specific notes:
- **Gas cylinder:** insist on a class-4 SGS-certified part. Class-3 cylinders fail after a few thousand cycles under heavier users.
- **Caster spider:** aluminium, 700 mm diameter — nylon spiders flex and lose alignment.
- **Mini pump:** look for "6 V mini air pump, 1.5 L/min" — anything smaller takes too long to reach lumbar pressure.
